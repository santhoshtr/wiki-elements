wiki-elements

wiki-3d-viewer

The wiki-3d-viewer element is a WebComponent to display 3D models in STL format.

Attributes

  • source: The URL of the 3D model in STL format.

Examples


                                            <wiki-3d-viewer source="https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:Redirect/file/Moon%20elevation.stl">
                                            </wiki-3d-viewer>
